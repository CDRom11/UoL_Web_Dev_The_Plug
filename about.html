About The GREAT Gaming Lounge

The vision of the Great Gaming Lounge is to become the only internationally competitive gaming lounge in Trinidad and Tobago. The lounge’s mission is to create a place for gamers that is owned, managed and run by gamers who will be able uniquely positioned to relate to customers. It will: provide service to and a location at which all gamers can meet and interact with each other comfortably, and safely; create opportunities for the development of gamers’ skills in order for participation in international gaming tournaments.
Only gamers can understand and provide service to gamers in a manner that truly embodies the enthusiasm that characterises gamers.
With the movement into local gaming journalism with our publication, The Plug, we hope to share news of our countries exploits and journey with local e-sports. It is through this gamers can have their progress and accomplishments be shown to the world, hopefully inspiring those that are interested in getting into e-sports to take that first dip.
